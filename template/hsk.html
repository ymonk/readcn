About HSK

HSK is the abbreviation of the Hànyǔ Shuǐpíng Kǎoshì (汉语水平考试 in Chinese, Chinese Proficiency Test or Chinese Standard Exam in English). It is China's only standardized test of Standard Chinese language proficiency for non-native speakers such as foreign students and overseas Chinese.
